About Fiman

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Fiman began as a simple idea: to create financial tools that are powerful yet accessible to everyone. We believe that managing your finances shouldn't require a degree in accounting or expensive software.

Our team of developers and financial experts came together to build tools that help you track expenses, calculate complex financial scenarios, and gain better control over your money - all with an interface that's intuitive and easy to use.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to democratize financial literacy. In a world where money management is increasingly complex, we provide the tools and knowledge to help you:

  • Track your spending effortlessly
  • Understand where your money goes
  • Plan for your financial future
  • Make informed financial decisions
